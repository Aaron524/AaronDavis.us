Handfeeding 100-Pound plus Tarpon


If given the opportunity, or rather just go ahead and make the opportunity; this experience is a must.

We started off purchasing 2 buckets of fish for $3 - mind you I wasn't sure what to expect when my girlfriend Chelsea said we were going to feed tarpon at Robbie's. The dock extends out into the Florida Keys water and as soon as anyone throws a fish you see a swarm of 100-pound plus tarpon swirl to the surface for a meal. Instantly my excitement level jumps and the GoPro is on, this is going to be a blast!

For more info on Robbie's check out their site where you can do everything from dinner to renting a boat : Robbies.com
